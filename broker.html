Are you an independent agent, general agent, or FMO looking to improve your product portfolio and better serve the Medicare eligibles in your area? Contact us to get more information on how you can become a representative for Arcadian Health. If you represent several regions across the United States, you may also have an opportunity to represent one or more of our markets. View our list of top producers.

Arcadian Health establishes and maintains collaborative business partnerships with our Brokers as well as and providing customized solutions to meet your business objectives. Arcadian Health's agents are certified in compliance with all applicable state laws as well as the regulations from the Centers for Medicare and Medicaid Serivces (CMS). Our customized certification and training process allows agents to spend their time selling the Arcadian Health plans family of products in the most efficient and effective ways possible.

Becoming a Broker with Arcadian Health

You must have an active license in every state in which you plan to enroll members, and you must certify with the local Arcadian Health's plan office for the current year. Please contact the local plan office or the corporate office with any questions about the certification process.

Services offered at your local Health Plan office

  • Information about getting certified online
  • Receive your writing number
  • Obtain pre-enrollment kits
  • Acquire web access to order materials from Direct Print Communications
  • Learn about approved educational tools in your local area
  • Submit completed enrollment applications.
