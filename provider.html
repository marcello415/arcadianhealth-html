Arcadian Health was founded by a physician and we are dedicated to the trust and security of a community-based, local network of care. Arcadian Health provides tools and services to help you meet your business goals and also serve your patient population. Whether you are part of a larger hospital network or a small, rural provider or group, Arcadian Health can help increase quality of care and services through our efficient business practices.

Our plans serve your patients

We are committed to rural markets and other underserved areas that do not have sufficient Medicare health plan options available. Since Arcadian Health offers only Medicare products, we specialize in serving just these populations and can focus on the needs of your patients. We contract through the Center for Medicare and Medicaid Services (CMS) to operate in our national network of plans.

Online tools help you get business done

Arcadian Health’s “AOS” software provides a secure web-based interface through which you will be able to verify eligibility, submit and lookup claims, and more.

Helpful staff are ready to assist

We have friendly, knowledgeable local staff dedicated to simplifying complex Medicare options for our members and dedicated Provider relations staff to answer your questions. We work directly with both members and providers on coverage and claims questions.

Contact Provider Relations

Please contact Provider Relations at 1-800-573-8597 for more information on becoming a part of the Arcadian Health Network.
