Important Notice

The Centers for Medicare & Medicaid Services (CMS), the federal agency that oversees the Medicare program, has placed Texas Community Care under marketing and enrollment sanctions. These sanctions began on December 5th, 2010 and are based upon marketing and compliance violations related to agent/broker misconduct. The suspension of all marketing activities for Texas Community Care will remain in effect until CMS is satisfied that the deficiencies that caused this suspension have been corrected and are not likely to recur.Texas Community Care takes this very seriously and has been working closely with CMS to address these sanctions. Texas Community Care is stillactively managing and coordinating the care of our existing members.


You will not be able to enroll in a Texas Community Care plan at this time.


If you have any questions, please call 1-800-MEDICARE. For more information about other plans offered in your area, call 1-800-MEDICARE or visit www.medicare.gov.
